How to make a PB&J sandwich

Step-by-step:

  1. Place two slices of bread on a plate or napkin.
  2. Apply jelly to right slice.
  3. Apply peanut-butter to the left slice
  4. Place the right slice on-top of the left, putting the jelly side against the peanut-butter side.
  5. Optional: Slice the sandwhich in half.
  6. Eat and enjoy!
